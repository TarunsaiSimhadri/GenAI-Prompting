subject : lamp review
Needed a nice lamp for my bedroom, and this one had additional storage and not too high of a price point. Got it fast.  
The string to our lamp broke during the transit and the company happily sent over a new one. 
Came within a few days as well. It was easy to put together.  
I had a missing part, so I contacted their support and they very quickly got me the missing piece! 
Lumina seems to me to be a great company that cares about their customers and products!!

subject : cofee maker review
Needed a new coffee maker for my kitchen, and this one had a sleek design and was affordably priced. It arrived quickly.
The first machine had a small leak, but the company was very responsive and sent a replacement right away. It arrived within a few days. Assembling it was straightforward.
I had an issue with the water filter, so I reached out to their support team, and they promptly sent the correct filter! BrewMaster seems to be a fantastic brand that genuinely cares about their customers and delivers quality products!!
